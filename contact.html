Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping policies?

We offer both domestic and international shipping. Domestic orders are typically delivered within 2-3 business days, while international shipping times vary by location.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer complimentary gift wrapping for all orders. You can add a personal message to be included with the gift.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For international orders, we also accept various local payment methods.
