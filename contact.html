Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping policies?

We offer both domestic and international shipping. Domestic orders are typically delivered within 2-3 business days, while international shipping times vary by location. For orders over ¥10,000, we provide free domestic shipping.

Do you offer gift wrapping services?

Yes, we offer complimentary gift wrapping for all orders. You can add a personal message to be included with the gift, and choose from our selection of traditional Japanese wrapping papers.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPay, LINE Pay, and bank transfers. For international orders, we also accept PayPal and various local payment methods.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of purchase, provided the product is unopened and in its original packaging. Please note that shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer unless the product is defective.

Do you offer sake tasting sessions?

Yes, we regularly host sake tasting events at our store. These sessions include sampling of premium sake, educational talks about brewing processes, and food pairing suggestions. Check our Events page for upcoming tasting sessions.

Can you ship alcohol internationally?

Yes, we can ship alcohol internationally to select countries. However, please note that international shipping of alcohol is subject to local customs regulations and duties. Contact us for specific details about shipping to your country.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk purchase discounts?

Yes, we offer special pricing for wholesale and bulk purchases. Please contact our sales team directly for wholesale inquiries and custom quotes. We also provide special rates for events and corporate gifts.

How do I join your loyalty program?

You can join our loyalty program by creating an account on our website or signing up in-store. Members earn points on purchases, get early access to special releases, and receive exclusive invitations to tasting events.
