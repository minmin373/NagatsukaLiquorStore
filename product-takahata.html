About This Wine

Takahata Winery's Oura Chardonnay 2022 is a premium Japanese white wine that represents the perfect harmony between traditional winemaking techniques and modern innovation. Grown in the cool climate of Yamagata Prefecture, this Chardonnay benefits from the region's unique terroir, resulting in a wine of exceptional quality and character.

The grapes are carefully hand-harvested and gently pressed to preserve their delicate flavors. The wine is then aged in French oak barrels, adding complexity and depth to its profile while maintaining the natural fruit characteristics.

Tasting Notes

Appearance: Bright golden yellow with green reflections

Aroma: Ripe pear, green apple, citrus zest, and subtle vanilla notes

Palate: Rich and creamy texture with balanced acidity, flavors of stone fruits, and a hint of minerality

Finish: Long and elegant with a touch of oak and refreshing citrus notes

About Takahata Winery

Founded in 1973, Takahata Winery is one of Japan's most respected wine producers. Located in Yamagata Prefecture, the winery is known for its commitment to quality and innovation in Japanese winemaking. Their vineyards benefit from the region's cool climate and mineral-rich soils, producing grapes of exceptional quality.

The winery combines traditional Japanese craftsmanship with modern winemaking techniques, resulting in wines that showcase the unique characteristics of Japanese terroir while maintaining international standards of excellence.
