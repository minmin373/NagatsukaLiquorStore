Yamazaki Distiller's Reserve

(4.5/5)
¥8,500

The Yamazaki Distiller's Reserve is a beautifully balanced single malt whisky that showcases the signature style of Japan's oldest distillery. This expression combines carefully selected malt whiskies aged in American oak, Spanish oak, and Japanese Mizunara oak casks, creating a complex and harmonious flavor profile.

  • Brand: Suntory
  • Category: Japanese Whisky
  • Region: Yamazaki, Japan
  • ABV: 43%
  • Volume: 700ml
  • Aging: Multiple cask types
  • Color: Golden amber
  • Nose: Raspberry, white peach, and Mizunara oak spice
  • Palate: Coconut, cranberry, and cinnamon
  • Finish: Sweet ginger, Japanese oak, and subtle smoke
In Stock

Key Features

  • Made at Japan's first and oldest malt whisky distillery
  • Uses pure water from the surrounding mountains
  • Combines whiskies aged in three types of casks
  • Perfect balance of fruit and oak flavors
  • Great for both sipping and cocktails
