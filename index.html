Frequently Asked Questions

What are your store hours?

Our store is open from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday through Saturday. We are closed on Sundays and national holidays.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services within Tokyo. Delivery fees vary based on location and order amount. Free delivery is available for orders over ¥10,000.

Can I return or exchange products?

We accept returns and exchanges within 7 days of purchase, provided the product is unopened and in its original packaging. Please bring your receipt for any returns or exchanges.

Do you offer gift wrapping services?

Yes, we offer complimentary gift wrapping services for all purchases. We have a variety of wrapping paper and ribbon options available.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our delivery partner's website.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination. Please contact us for specific details about shipping to your country.

Can I pre-order limited edition products?

Yes, we accept pre-orders for limited edition products. Please contact us in advance to secure your order. A deposit may be required for certain high-value items.
